Recent News:

14/11/2013:

Master’s student Tuğba Özdemir presented the proceedings paper “The impact of fire scenarios to the collapse of a tall structure” at 2013 Fire Security Symposium and Exhibition held 14-15 November 2013 in Istanbul, Turkey. The exhibition is organized by TUYAK - Turkish Fire Protection and Education Foundation. The conference proceedings paper can be downloaded in Publications section.

20/09/2013:

Serdar Selamet was invited to Nanjang Technological University (NTU) in Singapore as a Visiting Scholar between 16-18 September 2013.

05/09/2013:

Serdar Selamet presented the proceedings paper “The impact of fire scenario to the collapse of a tall structure” at SEMC Fifth International Conference on Structural Engineering, Mechanics and Computation held 2-4 September 2013 in University of Cape Town, South Africa. The conference proceedings paper can be downloaded in Publications section.

05/05/2013:

Serdar Selamet was accepted to ASCE/SEI Fire Protection Technical Committee. The committee meeting and ASCE Structures Congress was held 2-4 May 2013 in Pittsburgh, PA U.S.A. “Symmetric and asymmetric collapse mechanisms of a multi-story steel structure subjected to gravity and fire” presented paper can be downloaded in Publications section.

21/04/2013:

Serdar Selamet was accepted to COST IFER Project of European Commision as the Managing Committee Member of Turkey. The committee meeting and Applications of Structural Fire Engineering (ASFE) were held 19-20 April 2013 in Prague, Czech Republic. “Fire performance of an unprotected composite beam with semi-rigid end restraints” presented paper can be downloaded in Publications section.
